Food & Beverage industry

In the food and beverage industry, Trisun provides a full range of citrus fruits (orange, tangerine lemon, lime, grapefruit, sweetie etc.), tropical fruits (pineapple, mango, passion fruit, guava, papaya etc.), deciduous fruits (apple, peach, pear, apricot etc.), tomatoes and others. Our product range includes concentrates, beverage bases, single strength juices, essential oils, canned segments, IQF, and cells. In addition, we supply tropical fruit products, pomegranate and tomato products. A variety of packages is available for standard, chilled or aseptic shipping.
